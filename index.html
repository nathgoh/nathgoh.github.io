I'm Nathaniel Goenawan, a Computer Science undergraduate at Stanford University. I hail from the great city of Kalamazoo, Michigan where I had the opportunity to go two seperate high schools at the same time: Kalamazoo Area Mathematics and Science Center (KAMSC) and Kalamazoo Central High School (KC). Essentially what is was that half of the school day, I would go to KAMSC where they covered all my STEM required courses and beyond and then the latter half of the school day would be at KC covering the rest of my high school class requirements.
